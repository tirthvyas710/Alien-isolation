About

Alien Isolation is a heart-pounding survival horror game that perfectly captures the terror and atmosphere of Ridley Scott's original 1979 film "Alien". Set 15 years after the events of the first movie, players take on the role of Amanda Ripley, daughter of Ellen Ripley, as she investigates her mother's disappearance. The game takes place aboard the desolate space station Sevastopol, where a deadly Xenomorph stalks the corridors. Unlike many modern horror games, Alien Isolation emphasizes stealth and survival over combat, forcing players to hide, run, and outsmart the perfectly adapted killing machine. The game's retro-futuristic art style pays homage to the original film's aesthetic, while its dynamic AI system ensures that each encounter with the alien feels unique and terrifying. With its masterful sound design, oppressive atmosphere, and relentless tension, Alien Isolation stands as one of the most authentic adaptations of the Alien franchise in gaming history.
